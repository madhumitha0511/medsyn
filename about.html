Our Story

Founded in 2018 by a team of ophthalmologists and AI researchers, Medsyn was born from a vision to transform eye care through technology. Our breakthrough came in 2020 with the development of our proprietary AI architecture for ophthalmic diagnosis.

Today, Medsyn technologies are deployed in over 500 clinics across 28 countries. We continue to push the boundaries of what's possible in eye care, driven by our mission to preserve and restore vision for millions worldwide.
